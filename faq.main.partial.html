FAQ

Here is a list of frequently asked questions about chess and our club:

Who can play chess?

+

Chess is a sport that has virtually no limits. People of any gender and almost any age can play chess.

At what age can a child play chess?

+

Children over 6 y.o. can study in our club. We welcome boys and girls.

Do you provide classes for professionals?

+

Yes, our club offers tournament preparation services for professional chess players.

How often are workshops held?

+

We regularly conduct master classes, at least once per month. We invite leading champions of the world of chess.

How many times a week are classes held?

+

Chess classes are held four times a week in the evening. However, the schedule may change, so it is better to stay tuned or contact us.
